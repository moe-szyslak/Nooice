How to use the bot


STEP 1: Find the bot: @nooiceBot




STEP 2: Start by sending your location to the bot.




STEP 3: The bot will respond with ATMs it found near you. Click on a location, you will get it on Google Maps.

Chat with bot on Telegram



List of Commands

/register

Register as a contributer.

This means whenever you send your location to the bot, It will ask you if you would like to register an ATM at that location.

/unregister

Unregister as a contributer.

This means whenever you send your location to the bot, It will NOT ask you if you would like to register an ATM at that location.

/date

Shows you today's date in Geez




How can you contribute?

Anyone can contibute to this awesome project, we have outlined the steps below.

STEP 1: Use the /register command to register.





STEP 2: Send us your location.





STEP 3: Tell the bot you want to register an ATM at the location.





STEP 4: Pick the Bank which owns the ATM. Then party!!
